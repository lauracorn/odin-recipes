Protein Balls

Description

This six-ingredient protein balls recipe creates treats that are perfectly poppable and sweet as can be.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups quick cooking oats
  • 1/2 cup chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup vanilla or chocolate protein powder
  • 1/4 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients.
  2. Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm enough to roll.
  3. Shape into 1 and 1/2 inch balls. Store in the refrigerator.
